Tools: Figma, Adobe AI & INDD

Target Audience: 13-25

This is the stylescape, at that time i didn’t know what does scatter pattern has anything to do with the news itself, why do in such direction? I question myself overtime, even to the point where i manage to finish the brand-style docs and yet i still unable to come into conclusion. At early stage things still look quite bleak not really quite piece together just yet. In the stylescape you able to see the inspo i am taking upon making the prototype.

The pieces started to connect, after i finish making the Site-map. Moreover, when i start doing the lofi stages all the way through Hi-fi stages. The scatter is to symbolize the imbalance and uncertainty toward how does Vtube can impact the Gen-Z in wanting to read or watch the news, moreover it also represent the aesthetic in today’s generation too. Use of the color palette is to match with video-games atmosphere, to captivate viewers eyes in wanting to watch the news.

Throughout the end of the progression, i encounter some challenges especially toward creating the ‘subtle-animation’. Such as for creating the image slider effect, and specific kind of hover effect. (Refer image down below).

Nonetheless, in the end I manage to figure it all out within a week of constant frustration on why would i go such lengthy process in wanting to add in subtle-details. Turns out in the end i manage to execute the project quite well.

I am quite pleased for my very first time of using Figma at that time, and my love for Figma has only just begin.
