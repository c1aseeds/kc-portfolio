A New Chapter in my life.

Kenny Ciaputra.

Born in the small town of Bogor, the 6th largest city in West Java, Indonesia, I grew up balancing the realities of city and village life. My childhood was marked by a blend of practicality and idealism, always aware of my limits but dreaming big to achieve the seemingly impossible.

In late 2019, I moved to New Zealand to chase my dreams. One of my passions, photography, guided me towards a career in design. To maintain a balanced lifestyle and avoid burnout, I volunteer at Equippers Church in New Zealand. This involvement not only enriches my life but also keeps me grounded.
